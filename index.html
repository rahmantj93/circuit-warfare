How to Play

Circuit Warfare is a cyberpunk RPG that blends narrative choice with turn-based combat.

Story

Read the text, then press Continue or a choice button to progress. Some choices branch the story and change your ending.

Combat

  • Attack — deal damage based on ATK − enemy DEF.
  • Defend — halve incoming damage this turn.
  • Special — spend a full Tech meter for a double-damage strike. Tech charges as you take hits.
  • Inventory — use Health Packs to heal, Tech Cells to charge your Tech meter.

Keyboard Shortcuts (in combat)

  • A — Attack
  • D — Defend
  • S — Special
  • I — Open / close Inventory
  • Esc — Close Inventory / How to Play
  • M — Mute / unmute sound

Saving

Your progress saves automatically after each action. Use Continue on the main menu to resume.

Goal

Defeat the Apex Guardian to complete the mission. Your choices and your remaining inventory at the end determine which of three endings you unlock.
